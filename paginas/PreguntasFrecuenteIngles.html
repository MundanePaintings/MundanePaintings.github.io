What is MUNDANE?

MUNDANE is a portal aimed at artists, painters, sculptors, photographers, and artists in general, professionals or amateurs, who want to exhibit and sell their works of art online.

How do I sell on MUNDANE?

  1. Register and personalise your profile.
  2. Add as many artworks as you want. Indicate a detailed description, the materials used, sizes and shipping costs.
  3. You will receive e-mails from clients interested in your works.
  4. Contact your customers by phone or email to coordinate with them the delivery of the item and the most convenient payment method.

Who can publish works in MUNDANE ?

The website is aimed at individuals, professional and emerging artists. Only one artist is allowed per account and each artist can only show their own work.

How are works published in MUNDANE?

The management of the published works is carried out by the artist himself from a panel in the exhibition.
The steps to publish a work by an artist are:

  1. Register as a MUNDANE user.
  2. Create your artist profile.
  3. Log in to your account and click on "upload artwork".
  4. Enter your work with a .jpg photo by filling in a web form

What services does MUNDANE offer to the artist?

MUNDANE offers free exhibition space to artists.

What kind of works can I sell on MUNDANE?

You can sell:

  1. Original artistic paintings (made by you)
  2. Original drawings (made by you)
  3. Digital art and artistic photography with an original design.

You cannot sell:

  1. Articles that infringe any copyright.
  2. Articles manufactured for mass sale.
  3. Resold or second-hand items.

What information do I have to provide to register?

In the case of an artist you must provide your email address and information that allows us to contact you when required.

What is the privacy policy for my data?

MUNDANE will never give out the contact information of artists. When a visitor is interested in contacting an exhibitor, he/she must do so through the portal by filling in a form. The data is protected and will not be passed on to third parties unless you have explicitly given your consent.

I am an artist, can I sell my work directly?

One of MUNDANE's bets is to offer the artist the possibility to exhibit his art directly. In this way MUNDANE is not responsible for the management of the sale, shipping and invoicing. The potential buyer will contact the artist directly and they will have to agree on the management of the sale of the artwork.

What are the recommendations to follow when selling a work of art directly?

The responsibility for the sales process lies entirely with the artist when making the direct sale. MUNDANE has no responsibility in this respect. MUNDANE expects the behaviour and professionalism of all its artists. Some recommendations that are recommended to be followed in the selling process by the artist are:

  1. Proper packaging of the work.
  2. Make an invoice for the payment of the work with information identifying the artist.
  3. Submit a certificate of authenticity of the work signed by the artist himself/herself.
  4. Take out insurance on the shipment of the work.
  5. Accept cash on delivery.
  6. Allow "always" the return of the work.

Can I be removed from MUNDANE?

If, when an artist behaves abusively, engages in improper or fraudulent practices, or does not comply with the terms and conditions, he/she will be immediately terminated. MUNDANE may initiate legal action and consider making the necessary information available to the courts when illegal, abusive or fraudulent practices are detected.

I am an artist and I don't want to worry about the management of the sale of the work, how can I do it?

MUNDANE does not manage the sale of the works and only puts the buyer in contact directly with the exhibitor or artist.
